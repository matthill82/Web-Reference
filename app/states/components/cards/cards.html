Component Overview

To initialize a card, apply the .stw-card class to a containing <div>. A card is made up of three sections, a header, a body, and a footer. The card header will get the .stw-card__header class. The card body will get the .stw-card__body class. The card footer will get the .stw-card__footer class.

The card body can consume either a Data Table or a Tile.

For available classes and options, see the card overview legend below.
